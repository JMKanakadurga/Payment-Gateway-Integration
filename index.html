Who we are?

The Hope Foundation is a non profit charitable organization with philanthropic goals aiming to improve the quality of life for the community and beyond.The Sparks Foundation is an organisation aiming to collaborate, and help students through various programmes to inspire and integrate them, using innovative ways.We believe children are gifts of god and any service to them is service to god. We are committed to providing everyone and opportunity to learn, grow and help others grow.We constantly seek opportunities and innovative ways to create value through the work that we do to further our mission.
